Banana Inc.

Who are we?

Formed in 2017, we, Banana Inc. , have been in the mobile phone selling business for just over a year but have managed to sell over 10,000 phones monthly to customers all over Sarawak. We mainly sell mobile phones from Apple, Samsung, Huawei and Vivo. Besides that, we are the biggest retail partner of Hoco; a mobile phone accessories manufacturer. We have retail outlets all across Sarawak to serve our customers well and we are continuously expanding our business to serve more customers. Therefore, we believe that the quality of service is our utmost priority.

Our Mission

As a young company, we are constantly trying to set new milestones to hit and that includes being the biggest mobile phone distributor in Malaysia. As much as we wish provide as much variety in our line of products, we would never forego the quality of service we give to our beloved customers.

Our Vision

We, Banana Inc., envisioned to be the number one mobile phone retailer in the minds of all Malaysians. We believe in good customer service and envisioned that our customers would feel a sense of comfort whenever they purchase a phone from our outlets.
